FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Why use WatchTower?
WatchTower's patent pending Health Score provides an accurate snapshot of your Company's current financial health. This score is derived from four Core Ratios.
Do I need technical skills?
All you need is access to the internet, and your login information. Financial Professionals and Entrepreneurs need access to a Company's finances.
Who gains value from this?
WatchTower provides valuable utility to Entrepreneurs, Venture Capitalists, Investors and Financial Professionals.
Can I add Data from the past?
Yes. WatchTower allows you to add which quarter and year the financial activity occured, gathers core ratio data, calculate and displays it. The raw data is available for download as well.
Why Financial Ratios?
Raw financial data can paint a detailed picture, and it is often subject to misinterpretation. WatchTower takes the guesswork out of deciphering complex accounting data.
