Watchtower Technologies, LLC Financial Information Privacy Policy

Overview: This document summarizes the Watchtower Technologies, LLC comprehensive information security program (the “Program”). In particular, this document describes the Program elements pursuant to which Watchtower Technologies, LLC intends to ensure the security and confidentiality of covered records, protect against any anticipated threats or hazards to the security of such records, and protect against the unauthorized access or use of such records or information in ways that could result in substantial harm or inconvenience to customers. The Program incorporates by reference Watchtower Technologies, LLC’s policies and procedures enumerated below and is in addition to any policies and procedures that may be required pursuant to other federal and state laws and regulations.

Scope of Program: The Program applies to any record containing nonpublic financial information about a subscriber or subscriber’s related businesses that have a subscriber relationship with Watchtower Technologies, LLC, that is handled or maintained by Watchtower Technologies, LLC or its affiliates. For these purposes, the term nonpublic financial information shall mean any information a subscriber or other third party provides in order to utilize the financial accounting software platform from Watchtower Technologies, LLC.

Elements of the Program:

1. Risk Identification and Assessment. Watchtower Technologies, LLC intends, as part of the Program, to undertake to identify and assess external and internal risks to the security, confidentiality, and integrity of nonpublic financial information that could result in the unauthorized disclosure, misuse, alteration, destruction or other compromise of such information. In implementing the Program, the Watchtower Technologies, LLC will establish procedures for identifying and assessing such risks in each relevant area of Watchtower Technologies, LLC’s operations, including:

Employee training and management. Watchtower Technologies, LLC’s will evaluate the effectiveness of Watchtower Technologies, LLC’s procedures and practices relating to access to and storage of financial information. This evaluation will include assessing the effectiveness of Watchtower Technologies, LLC’s current policies and procedures in this area.

Information Systems and Information Processing and Disposal. Watchtower Technologies, LLC’s will assess the risks to nonpublic financial information associated with Watchtower Technologies, LLC’s information systems, including network and software design, information processing, and the storage, transmission and disposal of nonpublic financial information. This evaluation will include assessing Watchtower Technologies, LLC’s current policies and procedures relating to acceptable use of Watchtower Technologies, LLC’s network and network security, document retention and destruction. Watchtower Technologies, LLC will assess procedures for monitoring potential information security threats associated with software systems and for updating such systems by, among other things, implementing patches or other software fixes designed to deal with known security flaws.

Detecting, Preventing and Responding to Attacks. Watchtower Technologies, will evaluate procedures for and methods of detecting, preventing and responding to attacks or other system failures and existing network access and security policies and procedures, as well as procedures for coordinating responses to network attacks and developing incident response teams and policies. In this regard, Watchtower Technologies, LLC assumes the responsibility for monitoring and participating in the dissemination of information related to the reporting of known security attacks and other threats to the integrity of networks utilized by Watchtower Technologies, LLC.

2. Designing and Implementing Safeguards. The risk assessment and analysis described above shall apply to all methods of handling or storing nonpublic financial information. Watchtower Technologies, LLC will, on a regular basis, implement safeguards to control the risks identified through such assessments and to regularly test or otherwise monitor the effectiveness of such safeguards. Such testing and monitoring may be accomplished through existing network monitoring and problem escalation procedures.

3. Adjustments to Program. Watchtower Technologies, LLC is responsible for evaluating and adjusting the Program based on the risk identification and assessment activities undertaken pursuant to the Program, as well as any material changes to Watchtower Technologies, LLC’s operations or other circumstances that may have a material impact on the Program.

Privacy Policy

It is WatchTower Technologies's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect while operating our website. Accordingly, we have developed this privacy policy in order for you to understand how we collect, use, communicate, disclose and otherwise make use of personal information. We have outlined our privacy policy below.

• We will collect personal information by lawful and fair means and, where appropriate, with the knowledge or consent of the individual concerned.

• Before or at the time of collecting personal information, we will identify the purposes for which information is being collected.

• We will collect and use personal information solely for fulfilling those purposes specified by us and for other ancillary purposes, unless we obtain the consent of the individual concerned or as required by law.

• Personal data should be relevant to the purposes for which it is to be used, and, to the extent necessary for those purposes, should be accurate, complete, and up-to-date.

• We will protect personal information by using reasonable security safeguards against loss or theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use or modification.

• We will make readily available to customers information about our policies and practices relating to the management of personal information.

• We will only retain personal information for as long as necessary for the fulfilment of those purposes.

We are committed to conducting our business in accordance with these principles in order to ensure that the confidentiality of personal information is protected and maintained. WatchTower Technologies may change this privacy policy from time to time at WatchTower Technologies's sole discretion.
